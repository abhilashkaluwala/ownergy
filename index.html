FAQs

I'm hesitant to rent my house to strangers.

In this, we've got your back. We conduct a thorough tenant background check to ensure that you are not renting your home to strangers.

I'm having trouble getting my rent paid on time.

Timely rent collection is a hassle for all property owners, but you don't have to worry about it because we handle rent collection and make sure you get paid on time.

My tenant calls every now and then to complain about repairs and other issues.

Repairs must be addressed promptly or you will be spammed by your tenant until they are repaired. Our company investigates all repairs and ensures that they are completed on time, so you don't have to.

When my tenant left, he left the property in bad condition.

You will no longer have to be concerned about property damage because we will conduct regular property checks to ensure that your property is safe. We will also provide vacating services to ensure that your tenant leaves your property in a timely and orderly manner.

Is it possible for me to conduct a background check on the tenant before renting out the house?

Before renting out your home, we will conduct a thorough background check on the tenant and obtain a police clearance.
